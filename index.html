Discover Our Story

Food is a lifelong relationship, and we’re here to celebrate and nurture this beautiful bond.
It’s a part of some of our happiest memories, late nights with friends, the first romantic drive, the on-top-of-the-world feeling after the promotion… And when there’s a plateful of yummy food, the night, the drive and the promotion become even better! Just you, your loved one and the aroma of hot, just off the stove Pulav or a platter of mouth-watering buttery Pav bhaji, delicious till the very last bite… n that’s not it, if u r lookin for variety u can check out our menu for a variety of chaats, sandwiches,chinese, south indian, punjabi food e.t.c.
That’s what we The Honest Group of Restaurants are all about: HONEST, good food cooked with love and simplicity. What you see is just what you get.

Our History

Welcome to the Honest family—you belong to it as much as we do!
The Honest Group of Restaurants started in 1975. By god’s grace and your continuous support, we have grown and reach to the heart of our customers. We pride ourselves on the honesty and quality of our food—whether you enter a takeaway Honest Restaurant or an air-conditioned one, you can be assured that our flagship products will taste just the same. It’s a guarantee—honestly!
Our flagship products, Pav bhaji, Pulav, Indian (Punjabi), Chinese, Snacks e.t.c have made us a leading player in the food & snacks sector in Gujarat, Rajasthan, Goa, Thailand, USA & Canada.
We thank you for your continued patronage. Here’s to a lifetime relationship!
